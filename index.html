About ZIVS

The Zambia Institute of Valuation Surveyors (ZIVS) is the sole regulatory authority for valuation and property professionals. Established under Act No. 9 of 2023, we set rigorous standards for property valuers, managers, administrators, and facilities professionals.

Mandate: Register professionals, issue licenses, enforce ethics, monitor compliance and promote excellence.

Act No. 9 of 2023

Our legal mandate ensures transparent, competent practice in the Zambian property sector.

What We Regulate

Property Valuers

Licensed valuation of land, buildings, and assets.

Property Managers

Professional management of residential & commercial property.

Facilities Managers

Strategic coordination of workplaces and infrastructure.

Land Administrators

Land governance, administration & development oversight.
